# 🛡️ Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) – Homepage

A modern and responsive homepage built for the **Institute of Digital Risk (IDR)** — an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on **digital, cyber, and AI risk**.

The website highlights IDR’s mission, services, and community through a clean, minimal, and tech-focused design.

---

# 🚀 Tech Stack

- ⚛️ **React (TypeScript)** – Component-based frontend development  
- 🎨 **Tailwind CSS** – Utility-first responsive styling  
- 🧩 **shadcn/ui** – Accessible and reusable UI components  
- ⚡ **Vite** – Fast development and build tool  

---

# ✨ Features

✅ Fully **responsive design (desktop + mobile)**  
✅ **Hero section** introducing IDR’s mission  
✅ **About section** explaining the institute  
✅ **Service pillars section** including:
- 🎓 **Academy** – Training & bootcamps  
- 💡 **Innovation & Incubation** – Developing new risk models & governance frameworks  
- 🏢 **Advisory Services** – Implementing digital risk frameworks  

✅ **Community section** describing who IDR serves  
✅ **Contact section** with call-to-action  
✅ **Sticky navigation bar**  
✅ **Smooth scrolling between sections**  
✅ Modern UI with **Tailwind + shadcn components**
